Our Mission

What is Student Mental Health?

Mental health shapes how we think, feel, connect, and make decisions. For university students, it's especially important—affecting academic success, friendships, and long-term growth.
Most common challenges:

  • Anxiety: Persistent, sometimes overwhelming worry or fear.
    Symptoms: restlessness, trouble relaxing, irritability, rapid heartbeat.
  • Stress: The body’s response to pressure. A little is motivating, but too much harms health and performance.
    Symptoms: feeling overwhelmed, headaches, sleep problems, mood swings.
  • Depression: Persistent sadness, low energy, and loss of interest.
    Symptoms: hopelessness, appetite or sleep changes, difficulty concentrating.
